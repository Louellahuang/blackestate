About

There’s Nicholas, he’s the winemaker and married to Penelope. who looks after the business and restaurant. With Pen’s paren’ts Rod and Stacey, they family owns Black Estate. Together they grow wine, talk to much about the weather, and fight the good fight.

We’re set on making our wines true to the place they grow. The winemaking is simple - good estate grown fruit, organic and biodynamic, handpicked , vineyard derived yeasts, no additives or fining, minimal sulphur at bottling and where possible, no filtration. Then we have character.
